My Story

Photography isn't just my profession‚Äîit's my passion. For over a decade, I've been capturing life's most precious moments through the lens of a classic camera, believing that every photograph should tell a timeless story.

My journey began with a simple film camera and an insatiable curiosity about the world around me. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a lifelong dedication to the art of visual storytelling, where every frame carries the weight of human emotion and connection.
